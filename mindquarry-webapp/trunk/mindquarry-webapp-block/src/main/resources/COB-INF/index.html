Mindquarry - Gemeinsam mehr erreichen

Mindquarry is here to make your daily work more productive. Click one of the buttons below to manage teams, files, tasks or browse the wiki. If you need help, you can always click the the help symbol at the upper right corner of the screen. The expand button at the upper left corner of the screen shows you a customizable list of small helper programs devoted to making you more productive.

The teams block allows you to see all the teams you participate in. Additionally you can add members to your teams and create new teams for new projects. The workspace management block stores all your documents and keeps a history of changes. The Wiki is your personal idea-store, blackboard and glossary. Share ideas with your colleagues and develop creativity. With the task management block you can see your todo list and assign tasks to your team members The conversation management block stores all discussions, chat archives and meeting minutes.
